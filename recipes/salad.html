Caesar Salad

Prepare a delicious Caesar Salad with a few ingredients.This recipe is perfect as a side. It can also be a great healthy lunch option. Making a Caesar salad is quick and tasteful.

    Ingredients:
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Crips Croutons
  • Caesar Dressing
  • Olive Oil
    How to Make:
  1. Rinse lettuce in cold water
  2. Chill lettuce before using
  3. Mix lettuce, top with croutons and parmesan
  4. Add olive oil to taste
  5. Optional: Ground pepper to taste
