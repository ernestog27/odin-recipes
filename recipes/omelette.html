Omelette Recipe

Have a great start of the day with a good breakfast. This Omelette recipe is delicious and will make you get creative and create your own styles. Use it as a baseline for a great omelette.

    Ingredients:
  • Two large eggs per omelette
  • Sea salt
  • Butter
  • Mozarella, Mexican blend, Cheddar or Monterey Jack Cheese
  • Optional: Bell peppers, onions, cherry tomatoes, avocado
  • Optional: Bacon, ham or sausages
    How to Make:
  1. Beat eggs and salt with a fork until the eggs are foamy
  2. Add butter to non-stick pan and have it at medium heat
  3. Add eggs and reduce heat to low
  4. Add chosen cheese, veggies and proteins
  5. Once cheese starts melting, fold the eggs into an omelette
  6. Let it cook until on each side is golden brown
  7. Add avocado and tomatoes on top and serve
